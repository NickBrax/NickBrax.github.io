NickOwnage: The Journey

My journey started on April 10th 2009 when I upload my first video on YouTube. I was just 13 years old and I was a very big fan of Grand Theft Auto. Now 13 years later I have generated over 151 thousand viewers in total on my Youtube. But there is one GTA V video that really stands out with over 21 thousand views and still being watched every day is GTA V - Benefactor Surano Vehicle Location. Warning! Loud noises! This is one of my shortest video's and shows the location for a vehicle needed for the Epsilon Program in GTA V.

After making over 1000 guides, montages, gameplaycommentaries and livestream video's on games like: FIFA, Elder Scrolls Online, Minecraft, Assassin's Creed, The Walking Dead and Call of Duty I stopped in 2016 with making video's on YouTube. This was the time I finished school and started to work as a Swimteacher/AquaLeisure Instructor and later Teamleader in a great concern in my hometown in Assen. It was a busy time and I couldn't find any time to continue one of my greatest hobbies which was gaming, livestreaming and making video's.

 

NickOwnage: The Comeback

After a few years of moments on starting making video's again the lockdown came in 2020. This event was the best moment to give myself a chance of enjoying my greatest hobby again. On June 26th 2020 I streamed for the first time on Twitch with streaming gear I still had from before. Within a few months I achieved the goals to join the Affiliate programm on Twitch. When this happened I had gotten the opportunity to expand my channel and reward my viewers with lots of extra's. Beside that I started making some money so I could invest that in new streaming gear and improve the channel. Every month we made improvements to emblems, emotes, my setup, streamextensions until we had everything we wanted.

The highlight of my career on Twitch came on April 18th 2021. That Sunday my real life/online friend Lars and I started a 24 hour Warzone livestream. We made a whole event out of it and it was an amazing experience with our great community. Lots of friends (online/offline)and family supported us that day. After streaming for more than 3 days a week during a period over 1 year life became busy again. Which resulted in my final stream on Octobre 14th 2021. It was a wonderful time full of making new friends, completing many goals and a lot of gaming! There isn't a day that I look back and I feel excited to start again. Maybe someday..
